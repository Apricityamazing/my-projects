Formula

The formula for Water is H2O

It is sometimes expressed as HOH to better represent the actual molecular form

Nutrients

Water does not contain any significant nutrients.

However, water can contain a trace amount of minerals such as:

  • Calcium
  • Magnesium
  • Sodium
  • Potassium
  • Chloride
  • Iron
  • Fluoride
  • Copper
Benefits

Drinking water offers numerous health benefits, including maintaining bodily functions, aiding digestion, boosting energy levels, and promoting healthy skin.

To learn more click here

Risks

Overhydration can lead to water intoxication or hyponatremia, causing symptoms like nausea, headache, and in severe cases, seizures or even death.

An adequate water intake is about 15.5 cups (3.7 liters) for men, and about 11.5 cups (2.7 liters) for women. This is not set in stone, and you can use this calculator see how much water you should drink per day.

Hydration

Proper hydration means consuming enough fluids to maintain the body's water balance and support its vital functions.

If you are not staying hydrated on every single day, you need to start hydrating more often. Getting a big water bottle is a good start.
